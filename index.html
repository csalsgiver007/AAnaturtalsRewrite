Intro

I have always felt a connection to nature. I love being outside and observing. Watching the birds, smelling the flowers, listening to the brook, touching the green grass, tasting the air. These things bring me solace and I would much rather be out in a field of wildflowers than behind a desk 9 to 5. My love of nature brought me to where I am now. I became more curious and I gathered information about those wildflowers I was smelling.

I started making my own salves and balms about 10 years ago. Recently, I thought "I needed to take this a step farther" and decided to take the A course in Herbalism. I signed up for a 1 year program and learned so much. Not only about the plants and their medicinal uses, but how to apply them to everyday situations. In January of this year I made the decision to open All Around Naturals. To be able to share my knowledge one herb at a time makes me so excited and eager to learn and share more.

All of our products are handcrafted in small batches. This allows me to put more focus and good intentions into each one. There are no artificial ingredients, fragrances or chemicals. I focus only on a handful of herbs at a time and I would never market anything I have not tested on myself.
